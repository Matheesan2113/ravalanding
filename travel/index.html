QHacks Travel Details


Buses

We will be providing the following bussing routes:

Bus #1: Round trip from University of Waterloo campus to Queen’s University
Bus #2: Round trip from University of Toronto campus to Queen’s University

The number of seats available for reservation on the buses assumes that: Students from University of Western, Wilfrid Laurier University and University of Waterloo will travel to QHacks on Bus #1.

Students from outside of Ontario, McMaster University, OCAD University, University of Ontario Institute of Technology, University of Guelph, York University, Ryerson University, University of Toronto and the Greater Toronto Area High Schools will travel to QHacks on Bus #2.

Bus registration is now closed. The first 48 students to register for their respective bus are guaranteed a spot. There will be a wait list for any registrants beyond that cap.

Travel Reimbursement

Reimbursements will be provided to a limited number of participants traveling from institutions that are not catered to by the aforementioned buses.

Applications for reimbursements are now closed. Acceptances will be sent out shortly after and QHacks will reimburse up to $40.00 for your trip.

If you have received an acceptance, you will then have 4 days to send the receipts of your purchased trip to jennifer@qhacks.io. After this deadline, your reimbursement acceptance will have expired.

When planning your trip, please arrange to arrive at Queen's University no later than 6:00PM on Friday, March 4th and leave no earlier than 5:00PM on Sunday, March 6th.
